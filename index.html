About

This site is a simple way to view and customize Ann Arbor restaurant rankings. The list you see is my (Obeida Iqbal) original ranking based on places I've tried.
You can create your own ranking by using the up and down arrows on each restaurant card. Press the arrows to move restaurants higher or lower until the list matches your preferences.
That’s it — browse the restaurants, adjust the order, and build a ranking that fits what you like!
